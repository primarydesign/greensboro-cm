Facts About Play

  • Physical Abilities

    Play helps develop hand-eye coordination, dexterity and overall physical health.

  • Reading

    Play helps kids learn to read at an earlier age.

  • Social Skills

    Play helps kids make friends and develop social skills that will benefit them into adulthood.

  • Vocabulary

    Play helps increase their vocabulary and deepens their understanding of complex words and concepts.

  • Creativity

    Play helps develop creativity and problem-solving skills.

  • Confidence

    Play helps increase confidence and develop better self-esteem in children.
